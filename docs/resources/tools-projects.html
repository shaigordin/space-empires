Placenames and Gazetteers

  • Pedersen ANE placenames
    Data: Pedersen, O. (2007). ANE Site Placemarks for Google Earth (Version 10) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.6384045
    Map: https://www.arcgis.com/apps/mapviewer/index.html?webmap=38be0d419eb4498d83bf7abb089ff2de

  • Cuneiform Inscriptions Geographial Site (CIGS)
    Data: Rattenborg, R., Johansson, C., Melin-Kronsell, N., Nett, S., Smidt, G. R., & Andersson, J. (2024). Cuneiform Inscriptions Geographical Site Index (CIGS) (1.7) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14568765
    GitHub: https://github.com/glow-gh/cigs

  • Mesopotamian Ancient Place-names Almanac (MAPA)
    Gordin, Sh. & Clark, S. (2022). Mesopotamian Ancient Place-names Almanac (v1.0) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.6411251

  • Pleiades is a community-built gazetteer and graph of ancient places
    https://pleiades.stoa.org/

  • Web mapping service GeoNames is geographical database covers all countries and contains over eleven million placenames that are available for download free of charge
    https://www.geonames.org/
